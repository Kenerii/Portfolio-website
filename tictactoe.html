Tic Tac Toe

Player X

Objective: To have a fun and interesting game to play on your phone rather than using a pen and paper. Having automatic response when winning or losing a game by being programmed on SVG.

Problem: Having the game show whos turn it is just incase the people playing forget.

Solution:Integrating whos turn it is on top of the game with also announcing who the winner is. Also having a “New Game” button to restart the game rather than refreshing the web page.
