Chinese Zodiac

Objective: Creating a Chinese Zodiac table and search bar through javascript for people to find out what zodiac sign they are.

Problem: Having people go through a hassle to search up their friends birthdays rather than having access to a chart and a search bar on your phone.

Solution: Creating the javascript through html with all the calculations done to have any year be searched up through the search bar.
